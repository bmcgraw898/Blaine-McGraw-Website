Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how blainemcgraw.com (“the Site”) uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information to site owners about how users interact with the site.

2. How I Use Cookies

This Site uses cookies only for:

  • Analytics & Performance — Cookies from Google Analytics help track how visitors use the Site, including:
    • Pages visited
    • Time spent on the Site
    • Browser/device information
    • Traffic sources
    Basic Functionality — Some cookies ensure the Site displays correctly on different devices.

No advertising, tracking across websites, or personalized marketing cookies are used.

3. Types of Cookies Used

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies allow the Site to function properly. They cannot be disabled because they are required for core features such as navigation and security.

3.2 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

These cookies collect anonymous statistical data to help improve the Site.

Examples include:

  • _ga
  • _gid
  • _gat

These cookies do not identify you personally.

4. How You Can Control Cookies

You have the right to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You may:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Use browser extensions that block analytics scripts
  • Opt out of Google Analytics using the official add-on:

Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on

5. Third-Party Cookies

This Site only uses third-party cookies from Google Analytics. No advertising networks, embedded trackers, or social media cookies are installed.

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

This Policy may be updated occasionally. Revised versions will include a new “Last updated” date at the top of the page.
